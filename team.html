The Team

The Sooner Racing Team is composed of more than 35 passionate students majoring across engineering, business, computer science, and liberal arts. We are one of the most diverse teams in the country, with 57% identifying as a minority, and 18% identifying as women. Our team is making strides in improving diversity in motorsports from the ground up. Members of our team live and breathe in our workshop. Together, as one united team, we hope to create a lasting legacy of innovation and success.
